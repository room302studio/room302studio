EJ Fox is a data visualization designer and developer. He started with static infographics, learned to code, and at NBC News helped create the modern version of the Big Board — still used in election coverage today.

He founded Room 302 Studio to take on bigger projects with people he likes working with. He draws a lot of inspiration from nature, ecology, and art.
